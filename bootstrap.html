3 ways you can take care of your dogs dental hygiene!

Brushing your dogs teeth

If your dog allows you to, you can find toothbrushes designed for dogs and brush your pups teeth at home! This will save you money and potential dental problems in the future that will cost much more than a little toothbrush!

Giving your dog dental chew toys

There are chew toys you can find in your local pet store or online that will scrub any unwanted tartar buildup off your pups teeth as they chew and play with it!

Getting your dogs teeth cleaned professionally

Some dogs are less inviting for you to shove a toothbrush in their mouth which is just fine! Veternarians can perform a professional cleaning just as we humans get at the dentist!
